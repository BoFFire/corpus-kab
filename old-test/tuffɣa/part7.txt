Akka ihi ara tesbeddeḍ Haṛun akked warraw-is d lqeddacen.
Akka i yexdem i setta-nni n igeḍman i d-iffɣen si lmeṣbaḥ.
Ixdem iɛmuden s usɣar n lkasis, yerna iɣumm-iten s nnḥas.
Ixeddamen i d-ixtaṛ Umeɣlal Ameɣlal inna-d i Musa: Muqel!
Skud ur newwiḍ ara, ur neẓri ara d acu ara nefk i Umeɣlal.
Sebɛa n wussan ara tettettem aɣrum ur nesɛi iɣes n temtunt.
Zzi-d i wedrar s talast akken ur d-ittqerrib ara ugdud.
Ur ttseǧǧid ara zdat n iṛebbiten-nsen, ur ten-ttɛebbid ara.
Ttbaxuṛ-agi ḥesbet-tten d imqeddsen, ɛezlet-tten i Umeɣlal.
Musa tekcem-it tugdi, inna: Slan akk medden s temsalt-agi!
Dda Ɛli isɛa-d: Karim akked Ḥamid, akken i llan iderman-nsen.
Ferɛun isawel i Musa, inna-as: Ṛuḥet Ad tɛebdem Ameɣlal.
Ad wen-ilin idammen-nni d limaṛa deg yexxamen anda ara tilim.
Tceggɛeḍ-asen-d urrif-ik am tmes, ičča-ten am wakken d alim.
At Yiraten xedmen akken, wa ijemɛ-d cwiṭ, wa ijemɛ-d aṭas.
Ma temmugreḍ-d azger neɣ aɣyul n uɛdaw-ik iɛreq, awi-as-t.
Ikacbaren-nsent akked yeɛmuden-nsent ad ttwaxedmen s lfeṭṭa.
Deg wass n usteɛfu, ur tesseṛɣayem ara times deg yexxamen-nwen.
Timleḥfin meṛṛa n ufrag ttwaxedment s lxiḍ n lfina yeẓdan.
Azekka-nni ṣṣbeḥ, d nnda i d-izzin i umkan-nni anda i llan.
Tigusa n tnezduɣt, tigusa n ufrag akked imurar-nsent meṛṛa.
Ad k-rreɣ d Illu ɣef Ferɛun, ma d gma-k Haṛun ad k-yili d nnbi.
Ass-nni dɣa, Ameɣlal issufeɣ-d At Jennad seg Uẓeffun, am lɛeskeṛ.
Ibgaytiyen i tettwalim akka, ur tettuɣalem ara ad ten-twalim.
Lemwaǧi beddent am leswaṛ,  qquren waman di tlemmast n lebḥeṛ.
S wakka  a ten-jeṛṛbeɣ, ad waliɣ m'ad tebɛen ccariɛa-w neɣ ala.
Mkul yiwen ad d-yawi i wat uxxam-is, akken i tella twacult-is.
Xdem-as iri ara yesɛun tardest, tezziḍ- as-d s izirig n ddheb.
Akka ara txedmeḍ ddaw n igeḍman n lmeṣbaḥ i setta yid-sen.
Ad txedmeḍ lmeṣbaḥ akked dduzan-is s uṭaluc n ddheb yeṣfan.
D aɛebbwaj n uẓeṭṭa ara tent-iẓḍen, ad tent-irqem s ixerruben.
Ameɣlal inna-d: A-t-an dagi umkan ɣer tama-w, qqim ɣef uẓru.
Xedmen-as sin n isura ɣef yiran n tuyat-is, icudd-it yes-sen.
Musa iḍegger asɣar-nni s aman, imiren aman-nni uɣalen ḥlawit.
Ameɣlal iɛawed-d asiwel i Musa, Ameɣlal inna-d i Musa: Muqel!
Nnanasen: A ken-iḥaseb Umeɣlal, iwala-d ayen akka i txedmem!
Iḥesnawen meṛṛa xedmen ayen i d-yumeṛ Umeɣlal i Meɛtub d Ferḥat.
Imaziɣen walan afus anezmar i d-iẓẓel Umeɣlal ɣef Waɛraben.
Azekka d ass n usteɛfu, d ass wis sebɛa yettwaɛezlen i Umeɣlal.
Leqwanen yeɛnan lḥeqq: ur tettɛawadeḍ ara lexbaṛ ur nṣeḥḥa ara.
Fkan-iyi-t-id, ḍeggreɣ-t ɣer tmes, dɣa yeffeɣ-d uɛejmi-agi.
Issemlal xemsa n tẓerbiyin akken, issemlal setta-nniḍen akken.
Ixdem ɛecrin n tencirin i uqiḍun ɣer lǧiha n Tuburet, s asammer.
Ɣer lǧiha n umalu, isɛa meyya n iɣallen i d-iḥǧeb s temleḥfin.
Mmesteqsan amek ttilin, imiren kecmen s aqiḍun n Aderbad.
Dɣa sbedden fell-as imeqqranen ara t-iɛetben s lecɣal iweɛṛen.
Imaziɣen ttmuqulen Aɛraben-nni yemmuten, ɣef yiri n lebḥeṛ.
Musa yekker netta d Yacuɛa, amɛiwen-is, yuli s adrar n Yillu.
Ɣer lǧiha n Semɛun, s asammer, ad isɛu meyya n iɣallen di teɣzi.
Tabburt n ufrag ad tili d ẓẓerb ara yesɛun ɛecrin n iɣallen.
Ad ilin tnac n yeẓra, akken i llan yismawen n leɛruc n leqbayel.
Aqendur-agi ad isɛu imi di tlemmast, anda ara yettɛeddi uqerru.
Ixdem s usɣar n uqeccuc tincirin i uqiḍun, dɣa yesbedded-itent.
Ɣer lǧiha n lɣerb, isɛa xemsin n iɣallen i d-iḥǧeb s temleḥfin.
Tnac n yeẓra i yellan, akken i llan yismawen n warraw n At Ɛrif.
Musa yekker-d isellek-itent seg-sen, dɣa yessew taqeḍɛit-nsent.
